Game Overview

Table tennis, also known as ping-pong, is a fast-paced sport played on a rectangular table with a net across the middle. Players use paddles to hit a lightweight ball back and forth. The game originated in England during the early 20th century and has since become popular worldwide.

Game Highlights

Some of the most memorable moments in table tennis history include intense matches at the Olympic Games and World Championships. Key players like Ma Long and Ding Ning have left a lasting impact on the sport.

How to Get Started

To start playing table tennis, you'll need a table, paddles, and a ball. Basic strategies include mastering your serve, practicing your footwork, and improving your reflexes.
